# Prompt Templates for AI Lead Generation

## Avatar Deep-Dive Prompts

Use these follow-up prompts to go deeper on specific avatar dimensions when needed.

### Pain Point Excavation
```
For [NICHE] professionals who [ROLE DESCRIPTION]:
1. What are the top 5 problems they face daily that cost them time or money?
2. What solutions have they tried and why did those fail?
3. What would they pay to make each problem disappear?
4. What emotional language do they use when describing these frustrations?
```

### Buying Trigger Analysis
```
For someone selling [PRODUCT] to [AUDIENCE]:
1. What specific events trigger them to search for a solution?
2. What is their typical buying journey timeline?
3. Who else is involved in the purchase decision?
4. What objections do they raise before buying?
5. What proof or social signals do they need to feel confident?
```

## Audience Discovery Prompts

### Newsletter Deep-Dive
```
List the top 20 newsletters that [TARGET AUDIENCE] in the [NICHE] space subscribe to.
For each newsletter include:
- Name and URL
- Estimated subscriber count
- Publishing frequency
- Sponsorship availability (yes/no/unknown)
- Estimated sponsorship cost
- Audience overlap score (1-10) with our target
```

### Community Mapping
```
Map all online communities where [TARGET AUDIENCE] actively participates.
Include:
- Facebook groups (name, member count, activity level)
- Subreddits (name, member count, daily posts)
- Discord servers (name, member count, activity)
- Slack communities (name, estimated members)
- Industry forums (name, traffic estimate)
- LinkedIn groups (name, member count)
Rate each community's partnership potential (High/Medium/Low).
```

### Influencer Identification
```
Identify the top 20 influencers and thought leaders that [TARGET AUDIENCE] follows.
For each include:
- Name and primary platform
- Follower/subscriber count
- Content format (video, podcast, blog, social)
- Engagement rate estimate
- Sponsorship/collaboration openness
- Estimated partnership cost range
```

## Partnership Outreach Templates

### Newsletter Sponsorship Pitch
```
Subject: Partnership opportunity — [YOUR PRODUCT] × [NEWSLETTER NAME]

Hi [NAME],

I'm a reader of [NEWSLETTER NAME] and love your coverage of [SPECIFIC TOPIC].

I run [YOUR PRODUCT], which helps [TARGET AUDIENCE] solve [SPECIFIC PROBLEM].
Our audience overlaps significantly with your readers, and I'd love to explore
a sponsorship or partnership.

Would you be open to a quick chat about what a collaboration could look like?
I'm flexible on format — dedicated send, sponsored section, or something creative.

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Facebook Group Collaboration Pitch
```
Subject: Value-add collaboration for [GROUP NAME]

Hi [ADMIN NAME],

I've been an active member of [GROUP NAME] and noticed how engaged your
[MEMBER COUNT]+ members are around [TOPIC].

I've created [FREE RESOURCE/TOOL/GUIDE] that I think would be genuinely
valuable for your community. Would you be open to me sharing it as a
resource? Happy to do it as a live session, PDF, or whatever format works best.

No hard sell — just want to add value and see if there's a fit for ongoing
collaboration.

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Podcast Guest Pitch
```
Subject: Guest pitch — [SPECIFIC TOPIC] for [PODCAST NAME]

Hi [HOST NAME],

Big fan of [PODCAST NAME], especially your episode on [SPECIFIC EPISODE].

I'm [YOUR NAME], [YOUR CREDENTIAL]. I'd love to share insights on
[SPECIFIC TOPIC] with your audience. I can cover:

1. [Talking point 1 — actionable insight]
2. [Talking point 2 — contrarian take or data]
3. [Talking point 3 — story or case study]

I've been a guest on [OTHER PODCASTS] and always focus on delivering
actionable value for listeners.

Would this be a fit for an upcoming episode?

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```
