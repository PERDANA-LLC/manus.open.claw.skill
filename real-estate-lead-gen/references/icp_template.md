## Lead Categories

### Category 1: Expired Listings
Listings whose contract with the listing agent has ended without a sale. These sellers are motivated but frustrated — prime targets for a new agent.

**Where to find:** StreetEasy (NYC), Redfin, Zillow, MLS expired feeds

### Category 2: FSBO (For Sale By Owner)
Homeowners attempting to sell without agent representation. They often underestimate the complexity and become open to professional help after 30-60 days.

**Where to find:** Zillow FSBO section, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, FSBO.com

### Category 3: Price Reductions
Properties with significant price drops (>5%) signal seller motivation and urgency. Multiple reductions indicate increasing desperation.

**Where to find:** StreetEasy price drop filter, Redfin price history, Zillow price change alerts

### Category 4: Pre-Foreclosure
Properties with lis pendens filings, notices of default (NOD), or scheduled auctions. Owners are under extreme time pressure.

**Where to find:** PropertyShark, ACRIS (NYC), county recorder websites, foreclosure.com

## Scoring Rubric

| Factor | Weight | Score 10 | Score 7 | Score 5 | Score 3 | Score 1 |
|--------|--------|----------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Motivation | 30% | Must sell (foreclosure, estate, divorce) | Relocating | Price reduced | Testing market | Just curious |
| Timeline | 25% | Within 30 days | Within 60 days | Within 90 days | Within 6 months | No timeline |
| Property Value | 20% | $2M+ | $1M-$2M | $500K-$1M | $300K-$500K | Under $300K |
| Engagement | 15% | Responded to outreach | Open house scheduled | Active listing | Social media post | No signals |
| Competition | 10% | No agent (FSBO) | Expired listing | Listed with small brokerage | Listed with mid-tier | Exclusive with top firm |

**Weighted Score** = (Motivation × 0.3) + (Timeline × 0.25) + (Value × 0.2) + (Engagement × 0.15) + (Competition × 0.1)
